Sendai Tanabata Fireworks Festival

📅 Aug 5 📍 Miyagi
Pre-festival event on a fixed date
As the pre-festival event of the Sendai Tanabata Festival, one of Japan's three major Tanabata festivals, it lights up the urban riverside with fireworks.

This large-scale fireworks display is held annually on August 5th as a prelude to the official Sendai Tanabata Festival the following day. The launch site is located along the Hirose River in Aoba Ward, Sendai City, with approximately 16,000 fireworks being set off.

The event features high-density rapid-fire fireworks and various creative pattern fireworks in urban green spaces. Being close to Sendai's bustling city center, the entire city is filled with a festive atmosphere that evening, with streets adorned with large Tanabata bamboo decorations and food stalls.

Visitors can take the Sendai City Subway Tozai Line to either Omachi Nishi Park Station or Kokusai Center Station, and reach the viewing area upon exiting; alternatively, it is about a 25-minute walk from JR Sendai Station. Major roads in the city center will be closed to traffic that evening, allowing for pedestrian access.
