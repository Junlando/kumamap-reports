Atami Fireworks Festival (Summer Edition)

📅 Jul 20 📍 Shizuoka
Held multiple times throughout the year, featuring a natural bass sound experience due to the basin and bay topography.
Experience theater-quality sound effects created by the mountainous coastal basin, with fireworks displayed throughout the year.

The Atami Fireworks Festival has been held since 1952 as a traditional event that takes place seasonally and irregularly throughout the year, with the summer shows in July and August being the most frequent. The fireworks are launched from a fixed location in Atami Bay, spanning from Atami Sun Beach to the nearby waterfront park.

Each show lasts about 20 to 25 minutes. Due to Atami's natural basin topography, surrounded by mountains on three sides and facing the sea, the sound of the fireworks creates a strong echo against the mountain walls, resulting in a deep bass vibration similar to that of a sports arena.

For transportation, take the Shinkansen or JR Tōkaidō Main Line to 'Atami Station' and walk about 15 to 20 minutes to reach the beach viewing area. Many weekends in the summer coincide with national holidays, attracting large crowds of tourists and visitors staying at hot spring inns.
