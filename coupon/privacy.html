Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Junlando (吉冉數位工作室) ("we," "our," or "the Studio") handles information related to your use of our website and mobile applications.

1. Principles of Data Collection

We do not proactively collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your legal name, physical address, or national ID number.

2. Technical Data & Analytics (Firebase)

To improve our service quality and optimize advertising performance, we utilize third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Firebase) to record basic anonymous technical data, including:

  • Browsing behavior (e.g., click counts, duration of visit, navigation paths).
  • Device information (e.g., OS version, hardware model, advertising identifiers like IDFA or AAID).
  • Conversion events (e.g., records of clicking the "Download" button).

3. Use of Information and Third-Party Sharing

The data collected is used solely for enhancing user experience, maintaining server security, and sharing with third-party partners (including but not limited to Google Ads and Meta Ads) for precise ad delivery and conversion attribution analysis. All shared data is encrypted (hashed) or de-identified to protect your privacy.

4. Data Retention and User Rights

Since the logs recorded by the Studio consist of anonymous technical data that cannot be linked to a specific individual, we are unable to provide services for deleting, modifying, or querying specific data records. Such technical data is automatically destroyed upon fulfillment of the analysis purpose according to Firebase system settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at: info@junlando.com

隱私權政策

本隱私權政策旨在說明 吉冉數位工作室 (以下簡稱「本工作室」或「Junlando」) 如何處理您在使用本網站及相關行動應用程式時的資訊。

1. 資訊蒐集之原則

本工作室不會向用戶主動蒐集任何直接識別身分之個人資料（如姓名、地址、身分證字號等）。

2. 技術性數據蒐集 (Firebase)

為了優化服務品質與廣告成效，本網站使用 Google Firebase 等第三方分析工具，紀錄基礎的匿名技術數據，包含：

  • 瀏覽行為（如點擊次數、停留時間、瀏覽路徑）。
  • 設備資訊（如作業系統版本、廣告標識符 IDFA/AAID）。
  • 轉換事件（如點擊下載按鈕之紀錄）。

3. 資訊使用目的與第三方分享

上述數據僅用於改善使用者體驗、維護伺服器安全，以及與第三方合作夥伴（包括但不限於 Google Ads、Meta Ads）共用以進行廣告精準投放與轉換歸因分析。所有共用資料皆經過加密處理（Hashing）或去識別化處理，確保無法追蹤至特定個人。

4. 資料保留與用戶權利

由於本工作室紀錄之數據為不具名之技術日誌（Logs），無法與特定自然人進行關聯與對應，因此無法提供特定資料之刪除、修改或查詢服務。相關技術數據將於分析目的完成後，依系統設定自動銷毀。

5. 聯絡我們

若對本政策有任何疑問，請聯繫隱私權負責信箱：info@junlando.com
