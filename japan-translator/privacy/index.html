Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to JUNLANDO Japan Menu Translator (the "App"), developed and maintained by Junlando. The App provides image-based text recognition, translation, and content interpretation features, including but not limited to restaurant menus, drugstore products, product labels, and Omikuji (Japanese fortune slips). Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos and Camera: With your permission, the App may access your camera or photo library to capture or select images for text recognition and translation.
  • Text Content: Text detected in images (such as menu items, product information, or Omikuji content) is processed solely to provide recognition, translation, and display results.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical and usage information (e.g., device type, OS version, app usage) may be collected to improve the App.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide image text recognition, translation, and content interpretation services
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To maintain and enhance service quality

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Some data may be transmitted to third-party services (such as OCR or translation APIs) solely for processing purposes.
  • Information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect our legal rights and usersâ€™ safety.

4. Data Retention

Images and recognized text are not stored long-term and are processed only as needed to provide the service.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
