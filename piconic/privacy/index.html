隱私權政策

生效日期：2026-05-01

歡迎使用 Piconic（以下簡稱「本 App」）。我們非常重視您的隱私。本隱私權政策說明您使用本 App 時，我們如何處理與保護您的資訊。

一、蒐集的資訊

  • 照片與圖片資料：當您使用照片編輯、拼貼、模板排版或匯出功能時，本 App 可能會存取您主動選擇的照片。這些照片僅用於您在 App 內進行編輯與匯出。
  • 裝置資訊：為了產品維護、錯誤追蹤與服務優化，我們可能透過第三方服務（如 Firebase）自動收集裝置資訊，例如裝置型號、系統版本、App 版本與錯誤紀錄。
  • 使用數據：我們可能收集匿名使用行為資料，例如功能使用情況、模板或濾鏡使用次數，用於改善 App 體驗。這些資料不會與您的個人身分連結。
  • 聯絡資訊：若您主動聯繫我們，可能會提供電子郵件等資訊，僅用於回覆您的問題或支援請求。

二、資訊使用方式

  • 提供照片編輯、拼貼、模板排版與匯出功能
  • 改善 App 功能、使用體驗與穩定性
  • 協助除錯與提升服務品質
  • 分析功能使用情況，以優化模板、濾鏡與操作流程
  • 當您主動聯繫我們時，用於回覆支援請求

三、資訊分享

  • 我們不會出售您的個人資訊。
  • 本 App 可能使用 Firebase、分析工具或雲端服務等第三方服務，僅用於提供必要功能、錯誤追蹤與服務改善。
  • 僅在法律要求或保障使用者安全與合法權益時，才可能依法揭露必要資訊。

四、資料保存期間

本 App 不會主動永久保存您選擇編輯的照片。您建立或匯出的作品主要儲存在您的裝置本地端。若未來提供雲端同步、帳號或備份功能，將於 App 內明確說明。

五、照片存取權限

本 App 僅會在您授權後存取您的照片，且僅用於您主動選擇的圖片編輯、拼貼、排版與匯出功能。

六、資訊安全

我們致力於採取合理的安全措施保護您的資料，但請注意，任何網路傳輸或儲存方式皆無法保證絕對安全。

七、您的權利

若您曾主動提供資料，例如透過電子郵件聯繫我們，您可隨時要求查詢、更正或刪除。請透過下方方式與我們聯絡。

八、兒童隱私

本 App 並非為 13 歲以下兒童設計，亦不會刻意蒐集兒童的個人資訊。如發現不當蒐集，請聯絡我們以刪除資料。

九、政策變更

我們可能依據服務調整更新本政策。若有重大變更，會透過本頁面或 App 內通知公告。

十、聯絡方式

若您對本政策有任何疑問，歡迎來信：
support@junlando.com

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Welcome to Piconic (hereinafter referred to as “the App”). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we process and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos and Image Data: When you use photo editing, collage, template layout, or export features, the App may access the photos you choose. These photos are used only for editing and exporting within the App.
  • Device Information: For product maintenance, debugging, and service improvement, we may use third-party services such as Firebase to automatically collect device information, such as device model, OS version, app version, and crash reports.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data, such as feature usage, template usage, and filter usage, to improve the App experience. This data is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Contact Information: If you voluntarily contact us, we may receive information such as your email address, which will be used only to respond to your request or support inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide photo editing, collage, template layout, and export features
  • To improve app functionality, user experience, and stability
  • To help diagnose issues and enhance service quality
  • To analyze feature usage and improve templates, filters, and workflows
  • To respond to your support requests when you contact us

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • The App may use third-party services such as Firebase, analytics tools, or cloud services solely to provide necessary features, crash tracking, and service improvement.
  • We may disclose necessary information only when required by law or to protect user safety and legal rights.

4. Data Retention

The App does not actively and permanently store the photos you choose to edit. Your created or exported works are mainly stored locally on your device. If cloud sync, account, or backup features are provided in the future, they will be clearly explained within the App.

5. Photo Access Permission

The App accesses your photos only after you grant permission and only for the images you choose to edit, collage, arrange, or export.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, please be aware that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

If you have voluntarily provided any personal data, such as by contacting us via email, you may request access, correction, or deletion of that data at any time by reaching out to us.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information. If such data is found to have been collected, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed based on changes to our services. Significant changes will be announced on this page or within the App.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@junlando.com
